What CivicLink Does
📱
Works on Any Phone
Smartphone app, SMS reporting, and USSD (*717#) so every Sierra Leonean can participate — no data required.
🤖
AI Intelligence Engine
Predicts floods, crime patterns, road failures, and infrastructure risks before they become crises — giving government intelligence, not just complaints.
🏛️
WanGov Salone Integration
Fully integrated with Government of Sierra Leone's digital portal — reports auto-route to the right ministry, agency, and officer.
📊
Full Accountability
Every report tracked from citizen to resolution. Public scorecards. Agency rankings. Open data API. Citizens rate government responses.
🌍
Offline & Low-Bandwidth
Reports saved offline and sync when connected. Available in English, Krio, Mende, and Temne. Built for African network realities.
Implementation Roadmap
1
Phase 1 — Freetown Pilot (Months 1–3)
Launch in Western Area with 5 agencies. Target: 500 reports, 80% resolution rate, government MOU signed.
2
Phase 2 — National Rollout (Months 4–12)
All 16 districts, 20+ agencies, full WanGov integration, SMS/USSD live. Target: 50,000 active citizens.
3
Phase 3 — ECOWAS Expansion (Year 2+)
Liberia, Guinea, Gambia. Africa's first multi-country citizen intelligence network.
